Investment Highlights

Colliers International, as exclusive advisor, is pleased to present the opportunity to acquire the fee simple interest in 1819 10th St (“The Property”) in Oakland, CA. “The Property” consists of the following:

  • 2 Separate Parcels – APN #’s 6-49-25-1 & 6-49-27-1 totaling 2.14 Acres
  • ± 55,000 square feet in three freestanding industrial buildings
  • Property is 100% leased to 3 tenants including California Waste Solutions (http://www.calwaste.com), and Trapeze Arts, Inc. (http://www.trapezearts.com/)
  • Term remaining:
    • California Waste Solutions - lease expires 4th quarter 2016, tenant wants to renew
    • Trapeze Arts - 5 year lease commenced July 2015
  • Annual NOI is approximately $330,000 with Significant upside potential



Property Details


The site is listed as an “Opportunity Site” within the Oakland Specific Plan and is ideally located along Frontage Rd. and 10th St; 10th St being one of only two streets with direct access to Frontage Rd. between 7th St and W Grand Ave. The site is also less than one mile to the Interstate 80 Freeway on-ramp at W Grand Ave. The Property is currently unentitled, but a prime candidate for redevelopment and will provide a new owner with substantial passive income from existing tenants while going through the entitlement process.
